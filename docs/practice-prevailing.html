Prevailing Wind

Understand round wind vs seat wind.

In many rule sets, the prevailing wind (round wind) changes after all players have had the East seat. Your seat wind is your position; the prevailing wind applies to everyone.

If the prevailing wind is East and your seat is South, which wind gives you bonus value?
