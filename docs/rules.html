Winning (Rule Differences)

Riichi (Japanese)

You must have a yaku (a scoring pattern) to win. Riichi is a declaration when you are one tile away.

Example yaku: a simple sequence or a dragon pung.

Chinese Official

You can win with many patterns, but you must reach a minimum points threshold (often 8 points).

Example: dragon pung gives extra points.

American Mahjong

Hands are built to match a yearly card of specific patterns. You typically cannot win without a card pattern.

The card defines the winning hand types each year.
