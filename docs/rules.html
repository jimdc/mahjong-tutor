Rules (How A Hand Plays)

How turns progress, how you win, and what calls do.

Objective

Complete a winning hand before anyone else. Most rule sets require four melds plus one pair. You win by drawing the final tile or claiming a discard.

Turn Flow

  1. Draw a tile.
  2. Check for a winning hand.
  3. Optionally call a set (rules vary by region).
  4. Discard one tile.

Calls (Claiming Discards)

Chow

Claim a discard to complete a sequence of three. Often only allowed from the player to your left.

Pung

Claim a discard to complete three of a kind. Usually allowed from any player.

Kong

Claim a discard to complete four of a kind. You usually draw a replacement tile.

Winning

When your hand is complete, you declare a win. Scoring depends on the rule set (Riichi, Chinese Official, American, etc.).
